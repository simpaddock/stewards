Automate anything

Why do anything per hand?

SimPaddock provides a comfortable solution for many administrative tasks, such as:

  • Standings update
  • News articles
  • Custom rulesets
  • Statistics
  • Votings (e. g. Driver of the Day)
  • Sign up and livery submission
  • Incident reporting
  • Number plate generation
  • RSS feed (important for Social Media automatization)

Core features

Always updated standings

Just upload the results into SimPaddock. Everything will be updated in seconds.

All out of the box

All common features are included in the base package.

Batteries included

Driver of the day voting, sign up, livery submission, race calendar: Anything is aboard. No batteries required.

Privacy is important

SimPaddock does not connect to third party services (such as Google) and a YouTube embedding is included.

Complete open source

We think SimRacing needs for management better and open solutions. And because of this, SimPaddock is Open Source Software.

SimPaddock Docker

SimPaddock is designed to be ran in Docker

Hosted SimPaddock

As the development advances and SimPaddock we will offer free and paid hosting of SimPaddock instances.
